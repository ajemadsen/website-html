About me

Hello! I am a Senior at BYU studying Computer Science, expecting to gradutate in 2024. I grew up in Alpine, UT (just 30 minutes north of Provo), and I have been living in Provo since 2018. I served an LDS mission in Wellington, New Zealand and Cincinnati, Ohio during the COVID pandemic. A little less than a year after returning home, I met my husband Carter! We were blessed to get married November of 2022, and now we live here in Provo with our almost 4-month-old aussie-doodle puppy, Willow.

I didn't have any background experience in the field, but I was able to catch on pretty naturally and I have been enjoying it. I have the amazing opportunity right now to be a Web Developer on a rapid app development in the Center for Teaching and Learning here at BYU. I've been able to get real world experience working with clients, implementing new features and processes and designing web pages to create a seamless user experience. This has been an incredible opportunity for me and I am excited to see what else is coming my way.
